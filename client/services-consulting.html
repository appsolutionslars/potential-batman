Professional Consulting Services

appSolutions provides customers with a variety of professional services ranging from consulting and support to complete application development, deployment and hosting. More details on each of the available services can be found from the links in the menu. For more details on specific services or services that are not detailed on our website, please contact us.

appSolutions provides several kinds of highly professional consulting services. Consulting services are available for direct customers as well as for customers of our partners. Consulting services can be provided as on-site consulting or as online remote consulting. Several of our customers are provided with consulting services through the use of VPN connections and Terminal Services access to their environments.

appSolutions can provide access to some of the most experienced consultants and developers in the world to assist customers in various activities related to Progress Dynamics™. We can assist in on-site consulting with the planning and implementation of Dynamics development, as well as complete outsourced in-house development of Progress Dynamics™ and Progress based applications

appSolutions is a partner with Roundtable Software, the developers of Roundtable Total Software Management System. One of the primary areas of expertise at appSolutions is assisting customers with the planning, implementation, customization and use of the Software Configuration Management (SCM) tool - Roundtable TSMS™. appSolutions can also provide custom training sessions in using Roundtable TSMS™ - either as specially written courses to suit exact customer needs, or standard training classes. Consulting services are available as direct services or as sub-contracted services with the local Progress Professional Services organization.

appSolutions has the infrastructure and expertise to be able to provide various application development services for the development of Progress-based solutions. Application development can be done in-house as complete development projects or as parts of larger projects.

Whenever possible, the Software Configuration Management product, Roundtable TSMS, is used to ensure secure and stable development environments for application development. This also makes it possible to use the Roundtable functionality for multi-site development and deployment. This is an important part of the Quality Assucrance efforts that we make on all the projects we work with. For more details on development services, please contact us.
